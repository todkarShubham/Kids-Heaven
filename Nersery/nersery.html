Kids Heaven is a community-based education centre located in the beautiful bushland setting of Tumbi

Our experienced and highly qualified educators give children the tools they need to develop lifelong skills and opportunities they need to explore, discover, learn and play—all in a safe and nurturing atmosphere. The children experience unique learning environments, such as a wonderful sensory garden and timber fort, which are designed to stimulate the senses and challenge their growing

For over 40 years, our preschool has encouraged and facilitated children to develop a love for learning and to prepare them for the transition to primary school and lifelong learning. Our preschool places a strong focus on engaging educational activities and connecting the children with the natural environment. We believe that learning by doing is a great way to teach children the fundamentals they need to start their education and excitement for learning.
